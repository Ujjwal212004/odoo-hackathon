FINAL QUALITY BAR

The finished work should feel like a product designed by a team with years of experience building enterprise software. Favor restraint over decoration, typography over ornamentation, and consistency over novelty. Every layout should look production-ready, polished, and scalable. Do not include animations, motion concepts, transitions, parallax effects, micro-interactions, or interactive prototypes—those will be implemented later in code. Focus entirely on exceptional visual design, information architecture, spacing, hierarchy, and reusable components.
